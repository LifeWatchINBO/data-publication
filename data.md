# Data publication guidelines

## General guidelines

Use `; ` (semicolon + space) as a delimiter.

Use `key=value` pairs where appropriate.

    sex: maleIndividualCount=41; femaleIndividualCount=37
    lifeStage: adultIndividualCount=2
